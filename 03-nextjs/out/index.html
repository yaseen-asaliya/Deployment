Investors & Governance

Building long-term value for our shareholders

Al Saif Gallery (Tadawul: 4192) is Saudi Arabia's dominant specialty retailer in household and kitchen appliances, with a 73-store network, SAR 758.8M in revenue, and approximately 88% proprietary brands.

A leading position in a growing Saudi retail market.

Since our listing on the Saudi Exchange (Tadawul) in 2022, we have continued to invest in our proprietary brand portfolio, expand our national footprint, and deepen our operational excellence.

This page brings together the disclosures, financials, share information and governance resources that our investors, analysts and other stakeholders need. All data below is sourced from our IR platform and updated in real time.

Why Al Saif Gallery

Market leader

SAR 758.8M revenue, proven profitability, and a disciplined capital allocation framework aligned with Saudi Vision 2030.
